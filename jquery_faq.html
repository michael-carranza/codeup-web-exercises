National Park Facts!!

The Largest National Park
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve, AK, at 13.2 million acres
Smallest National Park
Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Memorial, PA, at 0.02 acres
What is the National Park System's Budget?
Fiscal Year (FY) 2014 Enacted: $2.98 billion
The nation’s deepest cave
1,593 ft deep in New Mexico at Carlsbad Caverns National Park
Yearly Attendance
In 2014, more than 290 million people visited the National Parks.
Total Acreage
51 million acres! About the size of Kansas.
The first national park
Yellowstone, was created in 1872 by President Ulysses S. Grant.
Caves as national parks?
Wind Cave National Park is the first cave to be named a national park
The only state without a national park or monument
It is actually the country’s first state, Delaware. Poor Delaware.
Super volcano in a national park?
The Yellowstone Caldera, in Yellowstone National Park, is a super volcano that is on pace to blow about 100,000 years from now.
